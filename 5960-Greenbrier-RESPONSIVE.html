Renovation Scope

Design Specifications

01
Glass + Aluminum Garage
Full-view glass garage doors in matte black aluminum replacing the existing carport. Interior-lit at night — the single move that signals luxury before the buyer reaches the front door.
02
Walnut Soffit + Cladding
Tongue-and-groove Ipe or walnut cladding on the garage fascia and entry zone. Warm amber tones against white brick. The MCM warmth signature that separates this from cold-dark competitors in the market.
03
Floor-to-Ceiling Windows
Existing small windows replaced with full-height black aluminum sliding glass doors and picture windows across the entire living zone. Indoor-outdoor living fully activated at the $1.9M price point.
04
Limewashed Brick Facade
Existing brick limewashed in warm white — preserving original texture while completely transforming the aesthetic. Clean, light, modern. Cost-efficient and the highest-impact exterior move per dollar.
05
Floating Concrete Approach
Large-format concrete pavers with grass-joint grid replacing the cracked existing driveway. Uplighting at grade. The arrival sequence sets the $1.9M tone before the buyer exits the car.
06
Luxury Interior Core
Open plan gut. White oak floors. Plaster walls. Calacatta marble waterfall island. Primary suite with soaking tub, stone tile, brass fixtures. Vittoria Casa staging opportunity on exit.
